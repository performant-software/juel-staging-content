Lundy, male (last name unknown). Owned by Joseph Sandridge (**[P47065](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47065 "P47065")**), he is hired out to the university in 1822: "Continued expenses for Joseph Sandridge for hire Lundy 67.50" (April 1, 1822). A relation of Joseph Sandridge, Richard Sandridge, also hired out slaves to the university in the same time period.
