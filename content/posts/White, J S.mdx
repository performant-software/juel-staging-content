JUEL ID/KEY: [P48851](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48851 "P48851")

"J. S. White," a photograph by Rufus W. Holsinger taken on March 20, 1919. 

The young woman in the portrait appears to also be in earlier Holsinger portraits labelled, "[Jessie White](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/1820 "White, Jessie")," taken on 1915 through 1916.

Additional information about J. S. White will be added as it is researched.

_NOTE that the name with which the portrait is labelled is not necessarily the name of the sitter in the portrait, unless otherwise verified (the date and name associated with each photograph is from the business ledgers of R. W. Holsinger)._![White, J. S.](https://iiif.lib.virginia.edu/iiif/uva-lib:1050752/full/561,778/0/default.jpg "White, J. S.")
