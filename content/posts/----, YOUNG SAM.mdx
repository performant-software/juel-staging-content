<p>Young Sam (last name unknown), adult male.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Arthur Spice Brockenbrough (A.S.B.) (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=43627" target="_blank" title="P43627">P43627</a>)&nbsp;was Proctor of the University of Virginia from 1819 to 1831. In this position, particularly during the years of the academical village's construction, he was frequently part of early transactions in hiring slaves, as detailed in his own records in the Proctor's Daybook. Most of the transactions detail his hiring slaves on behalf of the university, while some record payments to overseers involved in procuring slaves for labor. Numerous entries were recorded in his Daybook from 1822 through 1827, involving the slaves <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/688" title="Aaron">Aaron</a>&nbsp;(1826-7), <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/684" title="Davey">Davey</a> (1823, 1825-7), <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/690" title="Dick">Dick</a>&nbsp;(1827), "hands" (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_cb?cb=931" title="BROCKENBROUGH'S HANDS">CB0931</a>)&nbsp;(1825), "<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/685" title="Harry ">Harry of A. S. B.</a>" (1822), <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/686" title="Henry">Henry</a>&nbsp;(1822, 1827), "<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/681" title="Jack">Jack of A. S. B.</a>" (1827), <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/689" title="John">John</a>&nbsp;(1827), <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/692" title="Moses">Moses</a>&nbsp;(1825), <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/691" title="Ned">Ned</a>&nbsp;(1827), <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/693" title="REUBIN">Reubin</a>&nbsp;(1825-6), "<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/682" title="Old Sam">Old Sam</a>" or Sam (1822-1827), "Young Sam" or "Sam jr." (1822-3, 1825-7), <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/687" title="Simon">Simon</a>&nbsp;(1823, 1825, 1827), and <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/694" title="WILLIAM">William</a> (1823, 1824).&nbsp;<br /><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The following entries record Young Sam's work at the University (listed in chronological order):</p>
<ul>
<li>"Hire of young Sam from March 24th to Oct. 1st, making a $16 per month including board - $96" (September 16, 1823)</li>
<li>"Hand hire for hire of young Sam 1 month + board $15 + 2 months $20 - $35" (December 29, 1823)</li>
<li>"Sundries draft to A.S.B. building expenses for his hire of Sam, Davey + Young Sam for building stables. . .from 30 Sept a[t] 10$ per month each - $30" (December 30, 1825)</li>
<li>"Building expenses Draft to A. S. Brockenbrough for hire of Sam, Davey + Young Sam From 1 January to 1 April at 10$ per month each 50 - Memorandum Davey + Sam quited work on the 11th April but as Davey was at work for SB a part of his time have only. . .$90" &nbsp;(April 27,1826)</li>
<li>"To A. S. Brockenbrough for hire of Sam Jr. $1.50" (January 3, 1827)&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
