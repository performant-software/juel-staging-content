<p><strong>JUEL ID/KEY: <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47012" title="P47012">P47012</a></strong></p>
<p>Fleming, male (last name unknown), sometimes called "boy Fleming." Mentioned throughout the Proctor's Daybook in entries for 1822 and 1823, he was hired out to the university by Joseph Brand (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=46991" title="P46991">P46991</a>) in accompaniment with <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/425" title="Sukey">Sukey</a>. For instance, a memorandum of hires made for 1822 lists, "Boy Fleming and Woman Sukey of Jo Brand - Bond given 65 a blanket with each."&nbsp;</p>
