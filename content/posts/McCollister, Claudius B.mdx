Eutsler Charlottesville City Directory 1914-15 Listing">William E. Eutsler</a>) in the&nbsp;<em>Charlottesville City Directory, 1914-15.</em></span></p><p><span style="font-size: small;">Likely he was assisting in setting up the studio, cameras, developing the film, assisting with the sitters, and even operating the camera himself (although Holsinger, as owner of the studio, would be attributed as the photographer, as is typical of the time period).&nbsp;</span></p><p><span style="font-size: small;">The<em>&nbsp;Charlottesville City Directory, 1914-15</em>&nbsp;lists "McCollister Claudius B, photo University Studio h[ome] 715 W Main" (p127).
