Frank, male (last name unknown), adult (presumably). He is mentioned once in the Proctor's Daybook as being hired out to the university by owner Margaret Poindexter ([P47092](/en/people/df0252da-545c-4867-afc2-4c189a2ac2f3 "P47092")). On February 24, 1823, the Proctor recorded, "Labour acct. draft to bills payable for hire of negroes - to Margaret Poindexter for hire of Frank $60 and [George](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/586 "George") ([P47104](/en/people/c1e82ec0-2475-4723-ae52-6f5fec44e5b6 "P47104")) $58.50," for one year's hire.
