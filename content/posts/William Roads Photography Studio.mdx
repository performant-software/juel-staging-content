William Roads studio is one of the earliest Charlottesville photography studios, with photographer Roads ([P48015](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48015 "P48015")) having images dating from the 1860s. The exact dates of operation are not yet known. Address not yet known.
