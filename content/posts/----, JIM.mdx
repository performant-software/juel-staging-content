Jim, male (last name unknown). Owned by Richard Price ([P47064](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47064 "P47064")), he is mentioned several times in the Proctor's Daybook from 1822 to 1825:

*   March 12, 1822: "Continued expenses for Draft Rd. Price hire \[of\] Jim 60.75$"
*   September 25, 1822: "To R. Price for hire of Jim this present year - 65.00$. Raglands Bib 2$"
*   February 27, 1823: "Labour acct. draft to Rd. Price for Proctor Bond for hire of Jim" (at 65.00$)
*   January 1, 1824: "Paid to Richard Price for hire of Jim, 60$"
*   January 1, 1825: "Paid to Richard Price for hire of Jim, 55$"
