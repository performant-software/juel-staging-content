The selections below are articles from the _Alumni Bulletin_, 1894 to 1915, that discuss the Civil War. Clicking on the links will take you to the full-text, digitized scans of the articles. **SEE ALSO** [The Confederate Alumni, June 1912 Image Gallery](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/732 "Confederate Alumni, July 1912 Image Gallery")

[![](https://core-data-tina-cms.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/juel-staging-content/image.jpeg)](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/734 "Detail, Confederate Alumni ")

The above photograph (_Alumni Bulletin_ July 1912), shows the Confederate Alumni who were able to attend the Reunion held June 10-12, 1912 for the "band of young scholars and patriots, who were both students and soldiers during the years 1861-5." From L-R: T. S. Beckwith 1862-3 ([P31655](/en/people/20c732a2-4560-4303-8665-b75fca3e8b06#m1 "P31655")); Charles Parkhill 1862-3 ([P42771](/en/people/1d12d69e-0a4b-4662-a6f7-5f10f2ca44fd "P42771")); G. D. Wilkinson 1864-5/1870-1 ([P22197](/en/people/42d9eff2-a0dd-4be1-8b19-2ab635d483f2 "P22197")); A. G. Hill 1860-1 ([P24475](/en/people/1ef1ce7a-494c-490d-a85b-04777af0d333 "P24475")); S. S. Green 1860-1 ([P28211](/en/people/a79b5564-2806-44e3-87d7-90e9e097b763 "P28211")); H. C. Michie 1860-1 ([P29431](/en/people/26adc2ee-2af7-4700-8985-1754831b2240 "P29431")); C. H. Withrow 1855-60 ([P35993](/en/people/7914cd3b-604b-4898-96b5-9dc64342871b "P35993")). Isaac K. Moran ([P47640](/en/people/2744336d-d5a1-4469-83f0-40d0856c060c "P47640")) was a Veteran and Bursar at the University, but not an alumnus (1897-1912). **NOTE:** Click [**here**](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/732 "Confederate Alumni, June 1912") for the full photograph
