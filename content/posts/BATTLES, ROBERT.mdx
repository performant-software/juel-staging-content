<p><strong>JUEL ID/KEY:&nbsp;<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47363" title="P47363">P47363</a></strong></p><p>Robert Battles was a freed man of colour who owned a small farm near the boundaries of the University. His name comes up several times in the <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/minishowcase2/" target="_blank" title="John Hartwell Cocke Papers">John Hartwell Cocke papers</a> surrounding discussions of the purchase of his property by <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/648" title="JOHN HARTWELL COCKE">Cocke</a> (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=43616" title="P43616">P43616</a>). A document signed and dated by Battles on 22 March 1825, for instance, records his having received "six hundred and sixty dollars" from Cocke, as outlined in the 8 March 1825 Deed.&nbsp;</p><p>&nbsp;</p><p><strong>PRIMARY SOURCE INFORMATION</strong>&nbsp;</p><p><strong>1.
