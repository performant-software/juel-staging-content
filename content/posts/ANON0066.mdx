Anon0066, child (gender, name unknown). Owned by Mrs. Mary S. Ross ([P43754](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=43754 "P43754")), anon0066 is mentioned in a Bill for doctor visits by Dr. John Staige Davis ([P43680](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=43680 "P43680")) in 1860: "Aug: 1st\-27th To twenty-six visits Sally ([P47987](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=P47987)), Sally's child ([P47988](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=P47988))." Mrs. Ross was Hotelkeeper for Hotel E ([PL8436](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_place?place=8436#m1 "PL8436")) in the Academical Village, from 1856-1878. 

The other servants of Mrs. Ross mentioned in the Bill were: [Sally](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/938 "Sally") (P47987), [Charles](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/940 "Charles") (P47989), and [Charlotte](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/937 "Charlotte") (P47990).
