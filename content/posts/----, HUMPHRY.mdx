<p>Humphry, male, last name unknown. He is mentioned alongside Primus (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47036" title="P47036">P47036</a>), both of whom were hired out to the university by James Nelson (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47034" title="P47034">P47034</a>)&nbsp;for 40.00$ each (January 1, 1824), and 35.00$ each (January 4, 1825). Likely they were also the "2 hands" of James Nelson hired for 35.00$ on February 18, 1826. Humphry is also mentioned in an entry noting the payment of $37.50 to "<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/626" title="BROCKMAN, JAMES">James Brockman</a> (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47205" title="P47205">P47205</a>)&nbsp;for hire of Humphry draft J. Nelson - Bond" (January 4, 1825).</p>
