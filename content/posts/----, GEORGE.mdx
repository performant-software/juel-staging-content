George, male (last name unknown), adult (presumably). He is mentioned several times in the Proctor's Daybook as being hired out to the university by owner Margaret Poindexter ([P47092](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47092 "P47092")). On February 24, 1823, the Proctor recorded, "Labour acct. draft to bills payable for hire of negroes - to Margaret Poindexter for hire of [Frank](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/585 "FRANK") ([P47103](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47103 "P47103")) $60 and George $58.50," for one year's hire. On January 1, 1824 is recorded, "To Margaret Poindexter for hire George," at $58.00. Finally, on April 8, 1825 is recorded, "Bills Payable for draft to H. Watson ([P47102](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47102 "P47102")) for hire of George of Margaret Poindexter," also at $58.00. H. Watson was also involved in the hire of Lucy J. Poindexter's slave, Barrett ([P47101](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47101 "P47101")).
