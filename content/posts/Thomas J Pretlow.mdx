<p>Pretlow, originally from Southampton, VA, was born in 1815 and was a student at Virginia from 1833-34. His profession is listed as &ldquo;farmer&rdquo; in census sources and he is thought to have owned a plantation in the southwest Virginia area. He sat on the Board of Visitors from 1865-1872.</p>
