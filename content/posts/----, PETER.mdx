Peter (last name uknown), male, adult, owned by Alexander Garrett ([P43628](/en/people/dbd71b26-9277-4c5d-81da-e1bbf042ea9a "P43628")), the Bursar of the University of Virginia from 1825 to 1851. Peter is mentioned once in the Proctor's Daybook: "Bills payable to Dr. A. Garrett, Bursar, for a draft for the payment of the hire of Peter for 2 years Bonds taken in - $98.25" (February 5, 1827).
