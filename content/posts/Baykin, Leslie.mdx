"Leslie Baykin," a photograph taken by Rufus W. Holsinger on August 24, 1918.   
  

Note the stylistic similiarity to the portrait labelled, "[John Baxter](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/1471 "Baxter, John")." 

Additional information about Leslie Baykin will be added as it is researched.

_NOTE that the name with which the portrait is labelled is not necessarily the name of the sitter in the portrait, unless otherwise verified (the date and name associated with each photograph is from the business ledgers of R. W. Holsinger)._
