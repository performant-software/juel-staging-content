Anon0036 (last name unknown), young male; described as one of "two boys" of owner Abiah Thorn ([P47125](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47125 "P47125")) in the Proctor's Daybook. An April 10, 1823 entry records, "Brick Acct. draft to Sundries to A. Thorn for hire of 2 boys from the 7th," while a September 30, 1823 entry records a total of $600 "To Abiah Thorn for hire of two boys 6 months at $50."
