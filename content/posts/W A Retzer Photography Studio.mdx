W. A. Retzer's studio is one of the earliest known Charlottesville photography studios, with photographer Retzer ([P48025](/en/people/714c1977-f087-40b3-bb57-76a29a87b41e "P48025")) having daguerreotypes from 1850. The exact dates of operation are not yet known. Address not yet known.
