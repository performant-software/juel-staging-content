"Beatrice Harris," a photograph by Rufus W. Holsinger taken on December 26, 1918.

The UVA Library Catalogue states, "negative X07218A depicts the same subject." Negative X07218A is labelled, "[Lula Ross](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/1218 "Ross, Lula")."

Additional information about Beatrice Harris will be added as it is researched. 

_  
NOTE that the name with which the portrait is labelled (or, labelled "untitled") is not necessarily the name of the sitter(s) in the portrait, but rather the name of the person who paid for the portrait when it was taken (the date and name associated with each photograph is from the business ledgers of R. W. Holsinger)._
