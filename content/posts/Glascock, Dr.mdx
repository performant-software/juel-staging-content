JUEL ID/KEY: [P48653](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48653 "P48653")

"Dr. Glascock," a photograph by Rufus W. Holsinger taken on March 30, 1914.

The Charlottesville City Directory, 1914-15 lists the following about a Dr. Samuel Glasscock (note the alternative spelling); this may or may not be the same person (to be determined): "Glasscock Samuel W, physician 315 E Main h\[ome\] 11th nr Free Bridge Rd" (p.90).

Additional information about Dr. Glascock/Glasscock will be added as it is researched.

_NOTE that the name with which the portrait is labelled (or, labelled "untitled") is not necessarily the name of the sitter(s) in the portrait, but rather the name of the person who paid for the portrait when it was taken (the date and name associated with each photograph is from the business ledgers of R. W. Holsinger)._
