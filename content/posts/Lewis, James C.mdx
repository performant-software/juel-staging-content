Lewis," a photograph by Rufus W. Holsinger taken on April 10, 1914.</p><p>A James Lewis is listed in the Charlottesville City Directory, 1914-15: "Lewis James, waiter h[ome] 510 13th N W, Preston Heights" (p. 124).</p><p>Additional information about James C.
