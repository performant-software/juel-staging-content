Anon0020 (last name unknown), adult male, owned by Charles Poindexter ([P47090](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47090 "P47090")). He is mentioned in a December 30, 1825 entry in the Proctor's Daybook: "Labour acct. draft to Charles Poindexter for this sum ($1.25) paid for a Hat in place of interest in Bond for negro for 1825."
