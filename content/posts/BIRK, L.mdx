<p>L. Birk is mentioned once in the Proctor's Daybook, in a brief entry from September 11, 1828. He was paid $7.50, "Labour account for cash paid L. Birk for 5 pair shoes." It is unclear whether he was white or African-American.&nbsp;</p>
