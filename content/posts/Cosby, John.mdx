JUEL ID/KEY: [P48635](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48635 "P48635")

"John Cosby," a photograph taken by Rufus W. Holsinger on September 27, 1917.  
  

A man named John Cosby is listed in the Charlottesville City Directory, 1914-15; it may or may not be able the same person photographed: "Cosby John T, waiter h\[ome\] 206 5th S W" (p68).  
  

Additional information about John Cosby will be added as it is researched. 

_  
NOTE that the name with which the portrait is labelled (or, labelled "untitled") is not necessarily the name of the sitter(s) in the portrait, but rather the name of the person who paid for the portrait when it was taken (the date and name associated with each photograph is from the business ledgers of R. W. Holsinger)._
