<p>John Brannon served on the Board of Visitors from 1864-1865. He was born in Lewis Co., VA in 1822. His parents both served in the Revolutionary War. Brannon studied at Winchester Academy, an elite classical school in Virginia and later studied law at Weston to complete his admission to the bar in 1847. He served as a judge from 1872-1880 and before that, a member and later a senator in the Virginia legislature as part of the Whigs from 1853-1865. He attempted to run for US Senate in 1884 and 1886 but lost both times as a Democrat. He is known as the &quot;most well-respected man&quot; in Lewis County history.</p>
