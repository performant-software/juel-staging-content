**JUEL ID/KEY: [P46978](/en/people/6a7d24d7-440f-46ea-bd36-ab04fae61fb3 "P46978")**

Albert, adult male (last name unknown) is mentioned in the February 2, 1831 Faculty Minutes. A servant of Mrs. Sarah Carter Gray ([P43865](/en/people/65c0fcb5-8795-4825-b850-2b641b15fb67 "P43865")), he is mentioned in a Faculty Minutes report of the conditions of the hotels (Mrs. Gray at the time being the hotelkeeper for Hotel E ([PL8436](/en/places/ed8eb486-7e3c-48cc-a16c-3e4824ad6924 "PL8436")). 

He was also mentioned in a March 30, 1831 Chairman's Journal entry: "Servants gambling. Servant, named [Thornton](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/395 "Thornton") ([P44757](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=P44757 "P44757")), who was whipped for theft, and turned away from the University, last session, was found by the Janitor, playing cards with Mrs. Gray's ([P43865](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=P43865 "P43865")) servant Albert ([P46978](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=P46978 "P46978")), and Mr. Brockenbrough’s ([P43627](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=P43627 "P43627")) man [Trimpson](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/834 "Trimpson") ([P47794](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=P47794 "P47794")). This affair was referred to the Proctor and Asst. Proctor."
