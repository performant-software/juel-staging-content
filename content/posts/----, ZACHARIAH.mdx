Zachariah (last name unknown), adult male. Zachariah is mentioned twice in the Proctor's Daybook. An October 8, 1821 entry describes payment to owner L.M. George ([P47026](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47026 "P47026")) for the "hire of laborer (Negro) \[Zachariah\] on account of...Hotel AA," ([PL8432](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_place?place=8432 "PL8432")) while a November 25, 1822 entry records "Hotel AA for the sum \[15.00$\] to Zachariah for digging foundation."
