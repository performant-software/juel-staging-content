Prince, male (last name unknown), is mentioned once in the Proctor's Daybook as being hired out to the university by owner Charles Poindexter ([P47090](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47090 "P47090")[](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47093 "P47093")). On February 27, 1823, the Proctor recorded the payment of $125: "Labour acct. draft to Charles Poindexter for the hire of Prince and Reubin ([P47097](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47090 "P47090")) this year." Charles Poindexter also hired out the slaves Ben ([P47095](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47095 "P47095")), Moses ([P47096](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47096 "P47096")), Stephen ([P47099](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47099 "P47099")), and Wyatt ([P47100](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47100 "P47100")) to the university from 1823 to 1827.
