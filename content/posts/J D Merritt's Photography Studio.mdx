J. D. Merritt ([P48024](/en/people/1c08cf06-b0f9-4d4d-a0e5-eef193b6396f "P48024")) was an early Charlottesville photographer. His photographs were labelled, "J. D. Merritt, Practical Photographer (at Frayzer's old stand), Charlottesville, Va" ([CB1034](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_cb?cb=CB1034 "CB1034")). The exact dates of operation are not yet known. Address not yet known. (Note: _Charlottesville City Directory 1914-15_ lists "Merritt Annie wid J H, 820 E Jefferson" - possibly Merritt).
