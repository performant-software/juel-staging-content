<p><span style="font-size: small;">JUEL ID/KEY:&nbsp;<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48198" title="P48198">P48198<br /><br /></a></span><span style="font-size: small;">William E. Eutsler was an employee of <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/1078" title="R. W. Holsinger">R. W. Holsinger</a>&nbsp;(<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48008" title="P48008">P48008</a>), working at Holsinger's photography studio in some capacity; dates of employment thus far are only known with certainty to be 1914-15, when he and only one other employee are listed (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/1160" title="Claudius B. McCollister">Claudius B. McCollister</a>) in the <em>Charlottesville City Directory, 1914-15.&nbsp;</em>Likely he was assisting in setting up the studio, cameras, developing the film, assisting with the sitters, and even operating the camera himself (although Holsinger, as owner of the studio, would be attributed as the photographer, as is typical of the time period).&nbsp;</span></p><p><span style="font-size: small;"><img src="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/sites/default/files/media/images/51190534_1430310279.jpg" title="William E. Eutsler Portrait, c. 1891-6, " width="250" height="390" style="float: right;" /></span></p><p><span style="font-size: small;">William Edward Eutsler (1859-1939) was a travelling portrait photographer, trained in Staunton, that was associated with the following Studios:&nbsp;</span><br /><span style="font-size: small;">- Aldhizer &amp; Eutsler Traveling Artists, Broadway, Virginia, c1880-c1884</span><br /><span style="font-size: small;">- W.E. Eutsler Traveling Artist, Virginia, c1884-1888</span><br /><span style="font-size: small;">- W.E. Eutsler, Roanoke, Virginia, 1888-1891</span><br /><span style="font-size: small;">- W.E. Eutsler, Danville, Virginia, 1891-1896</span><br /><span style="font-size: small;">- Eutsler Brothers, Danville, Virginia, 1896-c.1905</span><br /><span style="font-size: small;">- S.L. Alderman, Greensboro, North Carolina, c.1905-aft.1908</span><br /><span style="font-size: small;">- Alderman &amp; Eutsler, Greensboro, North Carolina, c.1910</span><br /><span style="font-size: small;">- Eutsler Studio, Greensboro, North Carolina, by 1911-1913</span><br /><span style="font-size: small;">- Holsingers, Charlottesville, Virginia (no date provided)</span><br /><span style="font-size: small;">(Find a Grave)</span></p><p><span style="font-size: small;">The<em>&nbsp;Charlottesville City Directory, 1914-15</em>&nbsp;lists "Eutsler William E., photo University Studio h[ome] 1200 Wertland" (p81). Living at the same address was Miss Isabelle Eutsler and Miss Louis E. Eutsler (below):</span></p><p><span style="font-size: small;"><img src="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/sites/default/files/media/images/Screen%20Shot%202019-01-18%20at%202.08.40%20PM.png" title="William E.
