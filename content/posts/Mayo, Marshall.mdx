JUEL ID/KEY: [P48657](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48657 "P48657")

"Marshall Mayo," photographs by Rufus W. Holsinger taken on March 15, 1919.  
  
The J. F. Bell Funeral Home database has the following information about a "Marshall Mayo": birth date unknown; birth place Charlottesville, Virginia; parents Theadeus Haden and Mary Mayo; died April 24, 1931 in Ivy, Virginia; place of burial Oak Union Cemetery; age 27.    

Additional information about Marshall Mayo will be added as it is researched. 

_NOTE that the name with which the portrait is labelled (or, labelled "untitled") is not necessarily the name of the sitter(s) in the portrait, but rather the name of the person who paid for the portrait when it was taken (the date and name associated with each photograph is from the business ledgers of R. W. Holsinger)._**![Mayo, Marshall](https://iiif.lib.virginia.edu/iiif/uva-lib:1050739/full/1000,1337/0/default.jpg "Mayo, Marshall")**
