Anon0038 (last name unknown), male adults, described as the "hires" and "two hands" of Richard Poindexter ([P47093](/en/people/2f446e0a-2b6a-495c-848f-98c04712d8ba "P47093")). An entry from February 18, 1826 states, "Bills Payable for draft to Richard Poindexter for draft for hires of 2 hands $110." A February 24, 1827 entry records the total of $98.37: "Sundries draft Bills Payable to R. Poindexter for Hire $98.37." Poindexter also hired out [Aaron](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/588 "AARON") (P47108) and [Wyatt](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/587 "Wyatt") (P47105) in the same time period.
