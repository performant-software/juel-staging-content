Malinda, adult female (last name unknown). Malinda is mentioned as the "woman" (servant) of Mrs. Matthews in the correspondence of John Staige Davis (**[P43680](/en/people/3a14cfdd-ac55-448b-83dc-43261a120517 "P43680")**).
