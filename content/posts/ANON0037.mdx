Anon0037 (last name unknown), young male; described as one of "two hands" of owner J. H. Woods ([P46989](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=46989 "P46989")) in the Proctor's Daybook. A memorandum (undated) for 1821 states, "September 27th J. H. Woods 2 Hands at 40 per day."
