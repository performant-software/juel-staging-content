<p>Charles L. Mosby (1807-1879) was a prominent member of Lynchburg and served on the Board of Visitors from 1865-67. He also served on the House of Delegates from 1846-1848 and was a well-known local attorney. He was close friends with Joseph C. Cabell as well as John B. Minor, for whom he wrote a recommendation for judgeship in 1866.</p>
