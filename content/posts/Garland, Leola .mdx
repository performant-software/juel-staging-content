Holsinger taken on September 26, 1917.</p><p>The young woman below looks similar to the younger girl, "<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/993" target="_blank" title="Lena">Lena</a>," photographed by Holsinger on January 4, 1912. W.
