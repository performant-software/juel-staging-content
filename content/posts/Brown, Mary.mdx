Note that one photograph is labelled, "Dora Mansfield."<br />&nbsp;</p><p>Note that the Library Catalog entry for the portrait states, "see also <a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/1551" target="_blank" title="Mansfield, Dora">Dora Mansfield</a>,"&nbsp;a portrait taken on the same date.
