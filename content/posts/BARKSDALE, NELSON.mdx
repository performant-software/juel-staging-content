<p>Nelson Barksdale, Albemarle county resident, is mentioned several times in the Proctor's Daybook, in entries that describe his role as overseer. A typical entry is one from October 30, 1822:&nbsp;"Sundries to N. Barksdale Labour acct. for hire of Laborers 1819 - of Nunn (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47527" title="P47527">P47527</a>), Boxley&nbsp;(<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=46994" title="P46994">P46994</a>), etc. 11.91$" (October 30, 1822).</p>
