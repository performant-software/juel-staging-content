Isacah (last name unknown), male, adult. As mentioned in the Proctor's Daybook, Isacah (sometimes spelled, "Isaah") was hired out to the university by owner John S. May ([P47164](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47164 "P47164")), along with David ([P47170](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47170 "P47170")). On January 4, 1825, an entry records, "Labour Acct. draft to Bills Payable to John S. May for the hire of David and Isacah - $100.00"; a January 8, 1826 entry records, "Sundries with Bills Payable Labour acct. of John May Isacah + Davey $100.00 Bond."
