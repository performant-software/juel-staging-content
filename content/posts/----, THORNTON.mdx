**JUEL ID/KEY: [P47993](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47993 "P47993")** 

Thornton (last name unknown), male adult. Thornton is mentioned as a servant in the estate of William T. McCarty, which was included in correspondence from John Staige Davis ([P43680](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=43680 "P43680")) to Dr. J. B. McCarty from 1869. Link to the full transcript below.

The other servants listed are: [Henry](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/942 "----, HENRY") (P47994), [Cupping](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/945 "----, CUPPING") (P47997), [Mason](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/943 "----, MASON") (P47995), and [Micajah](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/944 "----, MICAJAH") (P47996).
