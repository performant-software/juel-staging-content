<p>Fleming Bowyer Miller (1793-1874) was born in Botecourt, VA and went on to become a longtime lawyer and politician. He served on the Board of Visitors from 1851-1855 after serving as a state representative from 1825-1837 and after time on the Board, US Attorney for the District of Virginia from 1853-1861. Miller also attended the Virginia State Convention of 1861. He attended Washington and Lee in college in 1812 before Litchfield Law in 1817. Miller also attended state conventions in 1829, 1830, 1850, 1851, and 1861.</p>
