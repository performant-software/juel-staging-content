<p>Robert A. Thompson (1805-1876) was born in Kanawha, Virginia (now West Virginia) in 1805. He sat on the Board of Visitors from 1851-1853 after studying law in his home state. He was a member of the State Senate from 1839-1846 before becoming a congressman from 1847-1849. He served in a variety of legal roles in California after this political career before his passing in 1876 in San Francisco, CA.</p>
