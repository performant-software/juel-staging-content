<p>Anon0045 (name unknown), adult male, described as the "single hand" of Dr. George Blaettermann (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=45632" title="P45632">P45632</a>). An entry from December 27, 1825 states, "Dr. Blaetterman for hire of hands from 21st to 28 November in all 12 days' work - single hand," at $6.00. Prof. Blaettermann, who lived in Pavilion&nbsp;IV&nbsp;(<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_place?place=8450#m1" target="_blank" title="PL8450">PL8450</a>) from 1825-1838, was known to have had several servants (as was common for occupants of the Academical Village).&nbsp;</p>
