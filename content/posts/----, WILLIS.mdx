Willis (last name unknown), adult male. Willis is mentioned twice in the Proctor's Daybook in 1823 and 1824. A September 30, 1823 entry states, "Brick Acct. Draft to Sundries to Peck & Crawford ([P47304](/en/people/657e8c9b-75a4-4dee-a564-c7bc4dbfa984 "P47304")) for hire of Willis 5 months ($40)," while a January 5, 1824 entry states, "Sundries draft to Peck & Crawford, Labour acct. for balance of Willis hire, $20."
