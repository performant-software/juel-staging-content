<p>JUEL ID/KEY:&nbsp;<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48804" target="_blank" title="P48804">P48804</a></p><p>"S. W. Gill," a photograph taken by Rufus W. Holsinger on July 16, 1913.</p><p>The UVA Library Catalogue describes the image as showing a porch with a machine for sorting apples. Of interest is the young African American boy standing to the right of the apparatus; he appears to be demonstrating the operation of the machine for the five men standing alongside him,.</p><p>Additional information about S. W.
