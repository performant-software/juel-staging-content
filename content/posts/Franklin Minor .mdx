	Franklin Minor 

author: >
	Philip Rubio (pmr2jq)

---
<p>Franklin Minor was born in 1812 in Albemarle County and graduated from Virginia. Minor was known for his activity in the Virginia Agricultural Society and the House of Delegates. Minor resigned from the House in 1863 and served on the Board of Visitors from 1859-1864.</p>
