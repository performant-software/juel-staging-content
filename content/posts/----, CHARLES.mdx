Charles, adult male (last name unknown). Owned by Mrs. Mary S. Ross ([P43754](/en/people/702be0ac-00c9-43fb-8182-22bb62243a3b "P43754")), Charles is mentioned in a Bill for doctor visits by Dr. John Staige Davis ([P43680](/en/people/3a14cfdd-ac55-448b-83dc-43261a120517 "P43680")) in 1860: "May 15th\-25th To seven visits servant Charles." Mrs. Ross was Hotelkeeper for Hotel E ([PL8436](/en/places/ed8eb486-7e3c-48cc-a16c-3e4824ad6924#m1 "PL8436")) in the Academical Village, from 1856-1878. 

The other servants of Mrs. Ross mentioned in the Bill were: [Sally](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/938 "Sally") (P47987), Sally's child [anon0066](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/939 "Sally's child") (P47988), and [Charlotte](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/937 "Charlotte") (P47990).
