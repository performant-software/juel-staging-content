<p>William Frazier (1812-1885), Board of Visitors member from 1864-1865, was born in Augusta Co., VA. He attended Yale in 1830 and left for Virginia to earn a law degree in 1834. Frazier served in the Virginia legislature, first as a member of the House of Delegates and then as a state Senator from 1861-1865. He died in Staunton, VA in 1885. He is most well-known for his early legal partnership with the Honorable John H. Peyton, a well-known attorney who kept in touch with Thomas Jefferson as the commonwealth&rsquo;s attorney.</p>
