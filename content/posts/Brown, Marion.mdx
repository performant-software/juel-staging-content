"Marion Brown," a photograph taken by Rufus W. Holsinger on July 20, 1912.

From 2 August 2019:  
  
"I think she is related to [Peggy Ragland Brown Spears](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/1182 "Spears, Peggy Ragland Brown")" ([P48231](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48231 "P48231")) - Ms. DeTeasa Gathers

Additional information about Marion Brown will be added as it is researched.  
  

_NOTE that the name with which the portrait is labelled (or, labelled "untitled") is not necessarily the name of the sitter(s) in the portrait, but rather the name of the person who paid for the portrait when it was taken (the date and name associated with each photograph is from the business ledgers of R. W. Holsinger)._
