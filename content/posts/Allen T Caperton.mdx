<p>Allen T. Caperton was born in Monroe, WV in 1810 and attended the University of Virginia as well as Yale College. Caperton served twice on the House of Delegates from 1841-1842 and later from 1857-1861. Between these terms, he was a state senator from 1844-1848 and eventually sat in the United States Senate from 1875-1876 as a Democrat. He died in 1876 in Washington. He was a stout Confederate and served in the Confederate Congress during the war.</p>
