Parr&nbsp;(<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48317" target="_blank" title="P48317">P48317</a>)&nbsp;and son Irving Parr (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48318" target="_blank" title="P48318">P48318</a>)&nbsp;were listed as owners:&nbsp;"Parr W J &amp; Son [William J and Irvine W], feed 400-404 E Market", "W J P &amp; Son" (p147).</p><p>Additional information about the store and the individuals will be added as it is researched.&nbsp;</p><p><em>NOTE that the name with which the portrait is labelled (or, labelled "untitled") is not necessarily the name of the sitter(s) in the portrait, but rather the name of the person who paid for the portrait when it was taken (the date and name associated with each photograph is from the business ledgers of R. W.
