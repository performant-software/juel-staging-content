<p>Douglas Gordon was a member of the Board of Visitors from 1862-1864 and a student of the University of Virginia from 1836-1837. He was born in Falmouth, VA, a small town north of Fredericksburg in 1818. He was a longtime farmer and died in 1882. He married his wife, Anne Eliza, in 1858 at Fredericksburg.&nbsp;</p>
