"W. A. Millner," a photograph by Rufus W. Holsinger taken on September 14, 1914.

The Charlottesville City Directory of 1914-15 has the following entry about W. A. Millner: 

"Millner William A., pres\[ident\] Charlottesville Colored Agricultural & Industrial Fair Association and supt. The So\[uthern\] Mutual Aid Assn." (p.137); "Charlottesville Colored Agricultural & Industrial Fair Association, 280 W Main, W A Millner pres, [Felix Bland](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/1305 "Bland, Felix") ([P48326](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48326 "P48326")) v-pres, J G Shelton sec-mgr, Charles Hopkins treas" (p.62).

The Directory also lists his home address: "home 329 Preston Ave" (p.137). 

See also the entry for [Horace H. Blunt](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/1474 "Blunt, Horace H."), agent for the Southern Mutual Aid Association. 

Additional information about W. A. Millner will be added as it is researched.
