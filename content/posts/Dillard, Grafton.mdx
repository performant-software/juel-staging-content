<p>JUEL ID/KEY:&nbsp;<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48659" target="_blank" title="P48659">P48659</a></p><p>"Grafton Dillard," a photograph by Rufus W. Holsinger taken on October 2, 1914.&nbsp;</p><p><br />A "Grafton Dillard" is listed in the J. F. Bell Funeral Home database; it may or may not be the same individual in question: "Grafton William Dillard, born December 18, 1898 in Plainsfield, New Jersey; died January 12, 1953 in Charlottesville, Virginia and buried at Oakwood Cemetery."</p><p>A "Grafton Dillard" is listed in the Charlottesville City Directory, 1914-15; it may or may not be the same individual in question: "Dillard Grafton, porter The Fountain h[ome] 10th S W" (p.74).&nbsp;</p><p>"The Fountain" was a soda shop/informal dining parlor in Charlottesville, located on West Main, as per the advertisment in the Charlottesville City Directory:&nbsp;<br /><br />"FOUNTAIN THE, soft drinks W Main nr Jefferson Park Av, M H Eskew mgr" (p.145).</p><p>More information about Grafton Dillard will be added as it is researched.&nbsp;</p><p><em>NOTE that the name with which the portrait is labelled (or, labelled "untitled") is not necessarily the name of the sitter(s) in the portrait, but rather the name of the person who paid for the portrait when it was taken (the date and name associated with each photograph is from the business ledgers of R. W. Holsinger).</em></p><p><img src="https://iiif.lib.virginia.edu/iiif/uva-lib:1042578/full/778,561/0/default.jpg" alt="Dillard, Grafton" title="Dillard, Grafton" width="778" height="561" /><strong>Sources</strong></p><p><span class="text_exposed_show">- "<a href="https://search.lib.virginia.edu/catalog/uva-lib:1042578#?c=0&amp;m=0&amp;s=0&amp;cv=0&amp;xywh=-1%2C-72%2C4019%2C2979" target="_blank" title="Dillard, Grafton">Grafton Dillard</a>," 1914-10-02 photograph in UVA Library Catalogue (X02649A)&nbsp;</span></p><p><span class="text_exposed_show"><br />-&nbsp;<a href="https://catalog.hathitrust.org/Record/101954103">Charlottesville City Directory, 1914-15&nbsp;[Hathi Trust]</a></span></p><p>&nbsp;<strong>&nbsp;</strong></p><p><strong>SEE ALSO</strong>:</p><p><a href="http://rwholsingerphotos.org/images.php" target="_blank" title="Holsinger Image Gallery of African Americans">Holsinger Image Gallery of African Americans</a></p><p>&nbsp;</p><p><a href="http://www2.vcdh.virginia.edu/afam/raceandplace/perl_scripts/funeral_search.html" target="_blank" title="J.
