Jacob, male (last name unknown). Owned by William Selden ([P47063](/en/people/b36c4622-f0cd-492d-bd58-11da1951a432 "P47063")), he is mentioned twice in the Proctor's Daybook, both in November 27, 1824 entries: "Labour acct. draft to Wm. Selden for hire of Jacob of Wm. Selden-" and "Wm. Selden to Labour acct. for 3 1/4 yards' cloth for Jacob...Leather for 2 pair shoes."
