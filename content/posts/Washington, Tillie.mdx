<p>JUEL ID/KEY:&nbsp;<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48992" target="_blank" title="P48992">P48992</a></p><p>"Tillie Washington," a photograph by Rufus W. Holsinger taken on January 04, 1913.</p><p>The gentleman in this photo is also seen in an later portrait taken November 30, 1918, labelled "<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/1932" target="_blank" title="Washington, Tal">Tal Washington</a>." &nbsp;The subject, however, looks to be about the same age, and even is very similarly dressed, in both photos. Likely there was an error in transcribing the date (1918/1913)&nbsp;and name.&nbsp;<br /><br />Additional information about Tillie / Tal Washington will be added as it is researched.&nbsp;</p><p><em>NOTE that the name with which the portrait is labelled (or, labelled "untitled") is not necessarily the name of the sitter(s) photographed, but rather the name of the person who paid for it when it was taken (the date and name associated with each photograph is from the business ledgers of R. W.
