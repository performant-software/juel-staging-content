<p>Stephen (last name unknown), male adult, is mentioned once in the Proctor's Daybook as being hired out to the university by owner Charles Poindexter (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47090" title="P47090">P47090</a><a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47093" title="P47093"></a>).&nbsp;A January 4, 1825 entry noted, "Labour acct. draft to Bills Payable to C. Poindexter for hire of Moses (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47096" title="P47096">P47096</a>), Reubin (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47097" title="P47097">P47097</a>), Ben (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47095" title="P47095">P47095</a>), Wyatt&nbsp;(<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47100" title="P47100">P47100</a>) &amp; Stephen (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47099" title="P47099">P47099</a>)&nbsp;$250 - bonded." Another slave hired out by Poindexter in this time period was Prince&nbsp;(<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47098" title="P47098">P47098</a>).</p>
