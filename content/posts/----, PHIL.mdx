<p><strong>JUEL ID/KEY:&nbsp;<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47075" title="P47075">P47075</a><a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47002" title="P47002"><br /></a></strong></p>
<p>Phil, male (last name unknown), is mentioned in several entries in the Proctor's Daybook regarding his being hired for the year (1822). He was owned by Twyman Wayt (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=44150" title="P44150">P44150</a>) (alternate spellings: Tayman, Wright, Wait).&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>November 25, 1822: "Bills Payable for the Proctors bonds for the hire of Negroes. . .To Twyman Wright for the hire of Phil 60$"</li>
<li>February 7, 1823: "60.00$. Sundries draft to A. S. Brockenbrough (<a href="http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=43627" title="P43627">P43627</a>) [Proctor] for this sum to T. Wayt for hire of Phil for 1822"</li>
</ul>
