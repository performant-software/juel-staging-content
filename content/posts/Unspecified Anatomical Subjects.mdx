Unspecified anatomical subjects, also known as "subjects," "cadavers," or "bodies," are frequently mentioned in the university correspondence regarding the procurement of bodies for anatomical study and dissection. The main body of correspondence is to and from John Staige Davis ([P43680](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=P43680 "P43680")), faculty member of the School of Medicine.
