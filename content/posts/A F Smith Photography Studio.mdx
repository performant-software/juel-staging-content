A. F. Smith ([P48023](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48023 "P48023")) was a Charlottesville photographer, active at least as early as the 1870's. He produced albumen prints labelled, "A. F. Smith's Photographic Temple of Art, Lobban's Building, Main St., Charlottesville, Va" ([CB1032](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_cb?cb=CB1032 "CB1032")). The exact dates of operation are not yet known; numerical address not yet known (Main Street).
