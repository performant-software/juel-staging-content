"Amanda Williams," a photograph by Rufus W. Holsinger taken on September 05, 1914. 

Additional information about Amanda Williams will be added as it is researched.

_NOTE that the name with which the portrait is labelled (or, labelled "untitled") is not necessarily the name of the sitter(s) photographed, but rather the name of the person who paid for it when it was taken (the date and name associated with each photograph is from the business ledgers of R. W. Holsinger).
