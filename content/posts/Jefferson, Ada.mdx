JUEL ID/KEY: [P48880](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=48880 "P48880")

"Ada Jefferson," a photograph by Rufus W. Holsinger taken on October 24, 1918.  

A portrait of a young boy of the same last name, entitled [Edwin Jefferson](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/1500 "Jefferson, Edwin"), was also taken on the same date; the two may be related, although more information is needed to verify).

Additional information about Ada Jefferson will be added as it is researched.

_NOTE that the name with which the portrait is labelled is not necessarily the name of the sitter in the portrait, unless otherwise verified (the date and name associated with each photograph is from the business ledgers of R. W. Holsinger)._ ![Jefferson, Ada](https://iiif.lib.virginia.edu/iiif/uva-lib:1049919/full/1000,1337/0/default.jpg "Jefferson, Ada")
