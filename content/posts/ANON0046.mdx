Anon0046 (name unknown), male adults, described as the "2 hands" of Branham & Bibb ([P47342](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47342 "P47342")) in the Proctor's Daybook. An entry from June 29, 1825 states, "Branham & Bibb draft to Labour acct. for hire of 2 Hands. . . 4 days each, $1, $8 \[total\]."
