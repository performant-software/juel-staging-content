**JUEL ID/KEY:** [P47281](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=47281 "P47281")

Jackson (last name unknown), adult male. Jackson is described in a Chairman's Journal entry dated October 5, 1863 as a "negro having a white wife" who lived on grounds: "Instructed Mr. Wm. A Pratt [(P43770)](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=43770#m1 "P43770"), Suptdr. of Grounds [(PL8688)](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_place?place=PL8688) & Buildings [(PL8638)](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_place?place=PL8638) to remove Jackson [(P47281)](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=P47281), a negro having a white wife, from the House he occupied on the University grounds [(PL8688)](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_place?place=PL8688)." The entry did not specify which house on campus he "occupied." An entry from October 10 noted, "Mr Pratt [(P43770)](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/public_person?person=P43770) reported that the above order had been carried out."
