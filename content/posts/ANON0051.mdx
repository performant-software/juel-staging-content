ANON0057, an unnamed group of "six boys." Owned by John Hartwell Cocke ([P43616](/en/people/c61406c9-c83a-4b8b-a093-dccb219d16cb "P43616")), they are mentioned in one entry in the Proctor's Daybook: 

*   September 30, 1823: "To hand for hire of [Charles](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/843 "CHARLES") ([P47524](/en/people/b53a971c-958a-4de9-af37-c0d6cb78afdc "P47524")) of J. H. Cocke at $12 per month, 6 mos., $72.00, and 6 boys (anon0051) ([P47526](/en/people/fdb9eef7-696f-42d6-bc29-fb700191a260 "P47526")) at $4.00 each per month, $144.00 (total, $216.00)"

Cocke was also paid for the hire of slave [Peter](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/664 "PETER") ([P47525](/en/people/d799e3a7-e5b0-4128-a383-03aacd8fcd73 "P47525")).

For more information about John Hartwell Cocke and other slaves associated with him, click [here](http://juel.iath.virginia.edu/node/648 "J. H. Cocke").
