Holsinger taken on September 01, 1915 and July 25, 1917.</p><p>Additional information about Lloyd Howard will be added as it is researched. Two photographs are dated September 1, 1915; one of the two is missing from the Library Catalogue (although likely it is a duplicate of the other). The third photograph is dated July 25, 1917.&nbsp;</p><p><em>NOTE that the name with which the portrait is labelled (or, labelled "untitled") is not necessarily the name of the sitter(s) in the portrait, but rather the name of the person who paid for the portrait when it was taken (the date and name associated with each photograph is from the business ledgers of R. W.
